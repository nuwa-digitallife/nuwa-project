## 1X TECHNOLOGIES - NEO Robot

### Funding & Valuation
- **Series A2** (Mar 2023): $23.5M → OpenAI, Tiger Global, Sandwater
- **Series B** (Jan 2024): $100M → EQT Ventures, Samsung NEXT, OpenAI
- **Total Raised**: $200M+
- **Seeking**: Up to $1B at $10B+ valuation

### Product (NEO)
- **Height**: 1.65m | **Weight**: 30kg | **Lift capacity**: 68kg+
- **Noise**: 22dB (quieter than refrigerator)
- **Market**: Consumer/home use
- **Price**: $20,000 (early access Oct 2025) or $499/month lease
- **Status**: Pre-order launched Oct 2025; ships Q3-Q4 2026 (US/Canada)
- **Important**: Requires teleoperator support for complex tasks (not autonomous)

### Alternative Product (EVE)
- **Type**: Wheeled android (industrial)
- **Deployment**: 50+ robots in Norway warehouse, 8-16 hours/day
- **Purpose**: AI training data collection

### Manufacturing
- **Primary**: Moss, Norway factory (co-located with engineering)
- **HQ Move** (July 2025): Consolidated to Palo Alto, CA (80,000 sq ft, 400-person capacity)
- **Purpose of Move**: Access Silicon Valley talent; manufacturing remains in Norway

### Strategy
- **Phased**: Commercial EVE (logistics/industrial) → Consumer NEO (homes)
- **Differentiation**: Consumer-first approach; emphasizes real-world home data for embodied AI
- **Key Hire** (2025): Vikram Kothari (ex-SpaceX VP Operations) → signals production scale ambitions

### Partnerships (VERIFIED)
- ✅ OpenAI (Series A2 & B investor)
- ✅ Tiger Global (Series A2 & B investor)
- ✅ Samsung (Series B investor, NEXT fund)
- ✅ EQT Ventures (Series B lead)
